Privacy Policy – Gather AutoBlock

This extension was developed to improve user experience in Gather Town by automatically locking tables to prevent unwanted interruptions. We value your privacy and are committed to maintaining a high level of transparency about how your data is handled.

1. Data Collection

Gather AutoBlock does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data.

  • No names, email addresses, IP addresses, or identifiers are collected.
  • No interactions, conversations, or personal content from Gather Town are accessed or stored.

2. Local Storage

The extension only stores a single setting: whether the auto-blocking feature is enabled or disabled. This is saved using Chrome's storage.local API and is not synced or transmitted externally.

3. Permissions

The extension uses minimal permissions strictly necessary for its functionality, specifically access to gather.town pages to detect and control table lock status.

4. Data Sharing

  • We do not sell or share any user data with third parties.
  • We do not use any data for analytics, advertising, or profiling.
